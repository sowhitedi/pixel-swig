    <pixel-swig> Photo Walk!

    May 13th at 5:00PM

    Downtown Jackson

    Aaron Phillips Photography will lead us
    on a photo walk around downtown Jackson.

    Photo Walk starts at 5 outside the King Edward Hotel

    It's a great chance to just play with color, your camera, & your friends—and to get a fresh perspective on our city. Also, you don't need to have a fancy camera or even anything more than a smartphone. Just bring your favorite image-making tool and have fun.

    <PIXEL-SWIG> is a social & professional gathering for illustrators, designers, & developers. It’s a happy hour series designed to bring creative professionals together in the same room, so we can enjoy beverages, swap stories, and make connections. We're all about an open, friendly creative community.

    This event series is created with love by: Christina Mattison (C. Mattison Illustration) and Rebecca White (So White Design & Illustration).
